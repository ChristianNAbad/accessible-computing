## The Biggest Shift in 30 Years

I've been writing code since 1995 — from C on Solaris to ColdFusion to modern TypeScript. I've lived through every paradigm shift: client-server, web 2.0, mobile-first, cloud-native. None of them compare to what's happening right now with agentic AI.

## What Is Agentic Coding?

Agentic coding isn't autocomplete. It's not GitHub Copilot suggesting the next line. It's **autonomous agents** that understand your entire codebase, make architectural decisions, write tests, and ship features — with you as the architect and reviewer.

With Claude Code, I'm orchestrating multiple agents that work in parallel: one building components, another writing tests, a third handling CI/CD configuration. The velocity is extraordinary.

## Real-World Impact: CannaBuddy.com

At CannaBuddy — the award-winning hemp THC beverage brand I co-founded — agentic AI workflows have been transformative. What once required a team of developers now gets done with one engineer and intelligent agents. The result: faster iteration, higher quality, and more time for strategic decisions.

## Key Principles for Agentic Development

1. **Architecture still matters** — AI executes faster, but bad architecture multiplies bad decisions
2. **Testing is non-negotiable** — Agents can generate tests, but you need to validate they test the right things
3. **Review everything** — Speed without review is just faster mistakes
4. **Leverage your experience** — 30 years of knowing what good code looks like is the secret weapon

## The Future Is Here

The developers who thrive in this era will be those who combine deep engineering experience with agentic AI fluency. It's not about being replaced — it's about being amplified.
